One in eight women in the United States will develop breast cancer in her lifetime.
Although less common in men, breast cancer can spread faster and more aggressively.
The second most frequently diagnosed cancer among women is breast cancer.
We are Pink Out at Ohio State.
This is our fourth year organizing the Pink Out event at The Horseshoe and raising funds for breast cancer awareness. Our goal is to give hope to those bravely fighting breast cancer that one day a cure will be found.
